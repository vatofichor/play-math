Solving Cases Rules


  • Law of Sines: SAA, ASA, SSA (ambiguous case)
  • Law of Cosines: SAS, SSS
  • Circumcircle diameter: 2R = a / sin(A)
  • Cos C = (a² + b² - c²) / 2ab
