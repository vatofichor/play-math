Likelihood Scale Guide


  • Impossible (0%): Cannot happen (e.g., probability = 0).
  • Unlikely (0% - 50%): Not expected to happen.
  • Even Chance (50%): Equally likely to happen or not (e.g., coin toss).
  • Likely (50% - 100%): Expected to happen, but not guaranteed.
  • Certain (100%): Guaranteed to happen (e.g., probability = 1).
