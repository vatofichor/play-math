What is a Stem-and-Leaf Plot?

Invented by John Tukey, the **Stem-and-Leaf Plot** is a visual table splitting double-digit numbers:

* **The Stem (Left):** Typically represents the tens digit.

* **The Leaf (Right):** Represents the ones digit, sorted sequentially.

It behaves like a horizontal **Histogram** but has a unique advantage: it **preserves the exact raw values** instead of hiding them inside bars!

Ogive Calculations

An **Ogive** (pronounced *oh-jive*) is a line graph representing cumulative frequencies.

* **Y-Axis:** Represents the **cumulative percentage** (from 0% to 100%).

* **Usage:** Find what score separates the top 20% of the class (the 80th percentile), or determine what percentile a score of 70 represents.

The Pareto Principle

Named after Vilfredo Pareto, the **80/20 Rule** states that roughly **80% of consequences come from 20% of causes**.

* **Bars (Left Axis):** Frequencies sorted in descending order of magnitude.

* **Line (Right Axis):** The cumulative percentage curve. Where this line crosses **80%** identifies the critical root causes to solve first!
