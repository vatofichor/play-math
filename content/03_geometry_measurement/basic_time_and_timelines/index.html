Reading Analog & Digital Clocks

Learn to tell time using the short hour hand (red) and long minute hand (blue). Adjust sliders to set hours and minutes, and read the time translated into words.

Spoken Time Translation

Interactive Clock Tips

* **Hour Hand (Red):** Points to the large hour numbers. Moves slowly as minutes pass.

* **Minute Hand (Blue):** Points to the outer tick marks (each number represents 5 minutes: e.g. 2 is 10 minutes, 6 is 30 minutes).

* **Spoken words:** We often read time relative to the hour, e.g. "quarter past" or "ten to".
