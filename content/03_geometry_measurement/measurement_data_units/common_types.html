Dimension Types

📏 Length (Distance):

Measures how long or tall. Common units: Inches, Feet, Miles, Centimeters, Meters, Kilometers.

⚖️ Weight & Mass (Heaviness):

Measures how heavy. Common units: Ounces, Pounds, Tons, Grams, Kilograms.

🥛 Capacity & Volume (Space inside):

Measures how much liquid it holds. Common units: Cups, Quarts, Gallons, Milliliters, Liters.

Real-World Benchmarks

• 1 Gram: Weight of a small paperclip.
• 1 Centimeter: Width of an average fingernail.
• 1 Liter: Volume of a medium water bottle.
• 1 Pound: Weight of a loaf of bread.
• 1 Meter: Height of a standard kitchen table.
