Comparison Rules Sheet

• Length/Height: Align starting points. The one extending further is taller/longer.
• Weight: Place on a balance scale. The side that tilts down is heavier. The side rising up is lighter.
• Capacity: Pour liquid from one to another. The one that holds more liquid has a greater capacity.
