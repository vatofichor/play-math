Key Proportions (Length)

US System:
  • 1 Foot (ft) = 12 Inches (in)
  • 1 Yard (yd) = 3 Feet = 36 Inches
  • 1 Mile (mi) = 1,760 Yards = 5,280 Feet
Metric System (Base 10):
  • 1 Centimeter (cm) = 10 Millimeters (mm)
  • 1 Meter (m) = 100 Centimeters = 1,000 Millimeters
  • 1 Kilometer (km) = 1,000 Meters
Approximate Conversion:
  • 1 inch is exactly 2.54 centimeters.
  • 1 meter is slightly longer than a yard (approx 39.37 inches).
