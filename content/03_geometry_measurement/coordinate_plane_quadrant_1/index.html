Understanding Grid Address

* **Origin (0,0):** The starting intersection where both axes meet.

* **X-Coordinate (Horizontal):** How far to walk **right** from the origin.

* **Y-Coordinate (Vertical):** How far to walk **up** once you've finished moving right.
