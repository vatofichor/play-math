Theorem Formulas Reference

• **Complementary:** $A + B = 90^\circ$
• **Supplementary:** $A + B = 180^\circ$
• **Vertical Angles:** $\text{Opposites are congruent}$ ($a = c$)
• **Triangle Angles:** $A + B + C = 180^\circ$
• **Parallel Transversals:**

Corresponding, Alternate Interior, and Alternate Exterior angles are equal. Consecutive Interior angles are supplementary.
