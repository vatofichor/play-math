Volume Packing Cheat Sheet

• **Volume Definition:** Space measured in cubes ($1\text{ unit}^3$).
• **Prism Layers:** $\text{Base Layer } (l \times w) \times h \text{ layers}$.
• **Exploding Composite Solids:** Counting layer by layer prevents double counting or missing internal cubes.
• **Fractional Cubes:** $0.5 \times 0.5 \times 0.5$ cube has a volume of $0.125$ (which is $1/8$ of a standard cube). It takes 8 half-unit cubes to form 1 standard cube.
