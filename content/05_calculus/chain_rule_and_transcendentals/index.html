Calculus Derivatives Rules


  • sin'(x) = cos(x)  |  cos'(x) = -sin(x)
  • (e^x)' = e^x  |  (ln x)' = 1/x
  • Chain Rule: dy/dx = (dy/du) * (du/dx)
  • Power rule: (x^n)' = n x^(n-1)
