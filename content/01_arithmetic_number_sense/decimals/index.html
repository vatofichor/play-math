Place Value Metaphor

* **$1.00 Dollar Bill:** Represents a single whole unit.

* **$0.10 Dime:** It takes **10 dimes** to make 1 dollar. That means a dime is **one-tenth** ($1/10$) of a whole.

* **$0.01 Penny:** It takes **100 pennies** to make 1 dollar. That means a penny is **one-hundredth** ($1/100$) of a whole.
