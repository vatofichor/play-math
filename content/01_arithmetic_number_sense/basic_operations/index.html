Geometric Projections

Exponent Sandbox (xⁿ)

Exponents represent repeated multiplication of a base by itself. But they also correspond directly to dimensions: a base raised to the power of 1 is a **1D Line**, power of 2 is a **2D Square Area**, and power of 3 is a **3D Cubic Volume**!

Exponential Equation

3³ = 27
Base (x) 3
Exponent / Power (n) 3
Repeated Multiplication Steps:
3 × 3 × 3 = 27
Dimension: 3D (Cubic Volume Grid)
