Standard Arithmetic Rules

1. Divide: Check how many times the divisor fits into the active value.

2. Multiply: Multiply the quotient digit by the divisor and write it below.

3. Subtract: Subtract the product to get the active remainder.

4. Bring Down: Pull down the next digit from the dividend.

If the remainder is not zero at the end of the last digit, it represents the leftover remainder ($R$).
