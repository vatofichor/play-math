What is a Ratio?

A **Ratio** is a mathematical comparison of two numbers. It shows how much of one thing exists compared to another.

* **Part-to-Part:** Compares two distinct categories directly. E.g., "For every 3 red apples, there are 2 green apples."

* **Part-to-Whole:** Compares one category to the **entire sum**. E.g., Red apples to total apples is **$3 : 5$** (or the fraction **$\frac{3}{5}$**).
