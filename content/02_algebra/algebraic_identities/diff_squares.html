Proof

Algebraic Expansion

Let's prove this algebraic identity by distributing the factors:

1. Start with the right-side factored expression:
(a - b)(a + b)

2. Distribute terms (FOIL method):
= a(a + b) - b(a + b)
= a² + ab - ba - b²

3. Since multiplication is commutative (ab = ba), the middle terms cancel out:
= a² - b²
