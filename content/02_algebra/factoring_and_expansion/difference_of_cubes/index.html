Step-by-Step Volume Audit

Trace the math of subtracting the smaller corner volume from the larger outer volume.

Volume Substitution


1. Compute individual volumes:
a³ = (3.2)³ = 32.768
b³ = (1.2)³ = 1.728

2. Compute difference (a³ - b³):
V_total = a³ - b³ = 31.040

3. Evaluate factored components:
(a - b) = 2.000
(a² + ab + b²) = 15.520

Factored product = (a - b)(a² + ab + b²) = 31.040

Algebraic proof

By expressing the volume subtraction geometrically: $$a^3 - b^3$$ We can slice the remaining shape into three rectangular blocks. Since each of the three blocks has a common dimension of $(a - b)$, we can factor $(a - b)$ out: $$\text{Volume} = (a-b)a^2 + (a-b)ab + (a-b)b^2$$ $$\text{Volume} = (a-b)(a^2 + ab + b^2)$$ This matches the algebraic difference of cubes formula!
